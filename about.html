Competitive Climbing

How it works!

Climbers are required to run a qualification round where they will be appointed which class you are in compared to other climbers.

  • Green: Beginners
  • Orange: Intermediate
  • Blue: Advanced
  • Purple: Pro

All climber will also have to pay an entry fee of $100

When competeing you will be judged on form, speed, and agility, to be able to get to the top and ring the bell first. First person to ring the bell wins the match. Although that does not mean you win the competetion! winning Climbers will then put in to our BOULDERING course where each climber will then display their spiderlike abilities by gripping up and over AND under our 125ft totem.

Things Climbers Should Bring

  • Water
  • Chalk
  • Climbing Shoes
  • Helmet
  • Great Attitude
